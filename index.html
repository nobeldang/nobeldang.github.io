About

Hi, I am a current Ph.D. student at Auto-AI Lab under Dr Bing Li interested in the field of 3D scene understanding and Autonomous AI. I did my master's at NYU in Computer Science. My research interests are in solving problems using Computer vision and Deep Learning techniques. Previously, I served as a Research Assistant at AI4CE Lab, NYU, under Dr Chen Feng solving problems related to Spatial Reasoning and did my master's thesis at VIDA Lab under Dr Guido Gerig to estimate the kinematics of wrist bone movement using geometric learning.

I am looking for highly motivated undergraduate and master's student (any college) to work on numerous projects like Semantic Segmentation, 3D Reconstruction of large-scale environment, Place Recognition and so on. Interested students please reach out to me on my email with your resume for prospective collaboration. Research experience with PyTorch and Deep Learning will definitely help students on being collaborators in my lab's projects.
